Key Team Members

Albertino Francisco

Chief Executive Officer

This person is the driving force behind the company, who makes things happen, put together the resources to support the company and take the product to the market place.

Stella Lima

Chief Financial Officer

This person's role is to look for investors and deal with banks, lenders, etc. She is also the Company's Controller, who is in charge of managing money and watching over the assets of the company.

Fernandes Lima

Chief Investment Officer

The CIO is in charge of preserving the company's asset portfolios. She generally supervises a team of qualified professionals and helps develop both short and long-term investment plans, recommends investments and assigns assets.

Boa Morte

Chief Marketing Officer

The CMO handles all aspects related to promoting and selling the Company's products and services.

Lima Francisco

Human Resources Manager

The Human Resources Manager plans, directs, and coordinates the administrative functions of an organization. He oversees the recruiting, interviewing, and hiring of new staff; consults with top executives on strategic planning; and serves as a link between the company's management and its employees.

Boa Morte Fernandes

Operations Manager

This is the leader for the operation with overall responsibility for the financial success of the business. She handles external relations with lenders, community leaders and vendors. Frequently, she also is in charge of either production or marketing for the business. She sets in motion the vision, strategic plan and goals for the business.
